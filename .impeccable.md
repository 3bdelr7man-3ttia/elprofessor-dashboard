## Design Context

### Users
ElProfessor founders and management use this dashboard to understand current financial health, assets, monthly burn, course performance, marketing spend, and future targets before connecting it to the core platform API.

### Brand Personality
Professional, legal-tech, confident. The interface should feel calm, credible, founder-focused, and useful for investor conversations without becoming a generic analytics template.

### Aesthetic Direction
Arabic-first management dashboard with a refined educational/legal identity. Use a crisp Arabic UI font, deep navy as the trust anchor, controlled gold accents, balanced light surfaces, and dense but readable operational layouts.

### Design Principles
- Separate operating truth from raw bank movement so leadership decisions use the right numbers.
- Keep forecasts and targets visible beside current reality to push toward break-even.
- Treat founder-owned assets and monthly asset rent as first-class financial data.
- Make AI advice ambient across the dashboard, not hidden on a final page.
- Prefer readable tables, compact KPI blocks, and decision alerts over decorative chart clutter.
